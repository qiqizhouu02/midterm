No Air

(2019) Made with wires, clay, and paper.

Is it seafoam or clouds that surround the lonesome figurine suppended in the center? I created this sculpture as means to explore the idea of being alone despite being surrounded by others. Judgement clouds your thoughts, which you tell yourself to not listen to. However, it's hard to not listen to the little voice in your head.
