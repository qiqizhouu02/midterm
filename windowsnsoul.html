Windows & Souls

(2019) Color Pencil 24 x 16 inches

In memory of my grandpa who took care of my grandma until his last breath. He passed away in May 2017. Dementia caused my grandma to barely remember anything. Yet she still remembers her husband. My family hasn’t told her of his passing in fear of the grief hurting her more. My heart breaks that she can’t recognize me, but it hurts more when I can’t tell her where Grandpa is. He passed two years ago but my memories of him are still very strong. He would always greet me with a hug and a smile and tell me how much I’ve grown. I wanted to immortalize a moment when he was happy. My worst fear is my memory of his face will start to change.
