Future Flowers

(2020) Made with LED lights, plastic water bottles, & wires

I aim to raise awareness for the excessive waste we produce because we consume more than we need. I tried to be sustainable as possible with this project by reusing plastic water bottles and an old frame of a painting. Each flower was made individually by melting plastic strips with a candle and hot glueing them together. The name “Future Flowers” conveys to the audience that the only flowers we will be able to give to our loved ones or friends in the future will be fake. There won’t be any of those pretty flowers if they are all destroyed by us.
